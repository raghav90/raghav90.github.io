Focus Areas

Research Interests

🌡️
Environmental Epidemiology
Mortality and morbidity outcomes from extreme weather exposures using large-scale Medicare data and distributed lag non-linear models.
🧠
Neurodegenerative Disease
Disease progression modeling for Alzheimer's disease and ADRD—integrating clinical, proteomic, and imaging biomarkers with event-based models.
⚕️
AI/ML in Healthcare
Scalable machine learning methods for biomedical heterogeneity, subtype discovery, survival analysis, and causal inference in clinical datasets.
🧬
Multi-Omics & Proteomics
Statistical harmonization and analysis of high-dimensional proteomics and genomics data for biomarker discovery in aging and neurological disease.
📊
Population Health & Exposome
Leveraging CMS Medicare and other large administrative databases to study how environmental and social exposures interact over the lifecourse.
🏥
Digital Health (India)
Building India-focused research on neurodegenerative disease, climate-health interactions, and digital health infrastructure at IIT Bombay's KCDH.
